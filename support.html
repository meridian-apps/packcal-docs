PackCal is a data field — here's how to use it

PackCal isn't a separate activity you start. It's a data field you add to an activity you already use:

  1. Install PackCal from the Connect IQ Store.
  2. On your watch, open an activity that involves carrying weight — Hike, Walk, or a native Ruck profile.
  3. Go into that activity's settings → data screens / fields, pick a field slot, and choose Connect IQ Fields → PackCal.
  4. Set your pack weight in PackCal's own on-watch settings (no phone needed). Start the activity — your load-aware calories appear in the cell.

After you save, the pack-aware totals also land in Garmin Connect under the activity's Stats → Connect IQ section.

Reporting a bug

Including the following helps me reproduce and fix the issue quickly:

  • Your Garmin watch model (e.g. Forerunner 965, Venu 3, fēnix 8).
  • Your watch firmware version. On most devices: Settings → System → About.
  • The PackCal version you're running, from the Connect IQ Store listing or the app's store details on your phone.
  • Which activity profile you added PackCal to (Hike, Walk, Ruck, …) and the field layout (1-field, 2-field, etc.).
  • What you did, what you expected, and what actually happened.
  • Roughly when it happened (date and approximate time).
  • If the activity saved, the Garmin Connect URL helps a lot.

Refunds

Garmin permits returns of paid Connect IQ apps (commonly reported as a 48-hour window). The mechanism is typically to uninstall the app from your watch shortly after purchase; Garmin then reverses the charge automatically. If you have trouble, contact Garmin Support. Meridian doesn't process payments and has no access to your purchase.

Looking for the full app?

PackCal is the lightweight option — it drops load-aware calories into your existing activity. If you want a complete rucking experience (multiple live screens, summary pages, lifetime totals, treadmill mode), see its companion RuckTrack.
